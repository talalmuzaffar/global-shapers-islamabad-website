Our Story

We are a vibrant community of young leaders from Faisalabad, united by a common purpose: to create positive change within our local communities. As part of the Global Shapers Community — an initiative of the World Economic Forum — we represent the voice and vision of youth in shaping a better future for our city and beyond.

Through our diverse initiatives, we aim to address pressing social issues, empower marginalized groups, and build a more inclusive and prosperous future for all. We connect with over 10,000 young leaders across 400+ hubs worldwide, bringing global perspectives to local challenges while ensuring our initiatives create real, lasting change.

Our History

2020

Hub Establishment

Global Shapers Faisalabad Hub was officially established as part of the World Economic Forum's Global Shapers Community.

2021

First Major Projects

We launched our flagship initiatives: 'Ilm ka Silsila' and 'Project Parwaz' to empower youth through education and mentorship.

2022

Scaling Impact

Our hub expanded its reach, serving over 3,000 community members and establishing partnerships with local government, NGOs, and educational institutions.

2023

National Recognition

Recognized as one of Pakistan's most active Global Shapers hubs, we hosted our first inter-hub collaboration.

2024-2025

Sustainable Growth

Today, we operate multiple ongoing projects, have impacted 5,000+ lives, and continue to grow our community of changemakers.
