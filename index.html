Our Purpose

We, the Global Shapers Faisalabad, take collaborative action to address important problems in our community. We do this through:

  • Collaborating on impactful projects that address real needs
  • Empowering youth through education, mentorship, and skill development
  • Advocating for climate action and environmental sustainability
  • Building inclusive communities that uplift the marginalized
Learn More

What is the Global Shapers Community?

Born out of the World Economic Forum, the Global Shapers Community is a network of inspiring young people under the age of 30 working together to address local, regional, and global challenges.
